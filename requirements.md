# PROJECT TITLE
Grameen Sathi AI

# PROBLEM STATEMENT
Despite having valuable products and skills , a huge section of rural entrepreneurs in India are unable to sell their products on Amazon due to low digital literacy , language barriers ,confusion
about price and logistics and most importantly from the fear of increasing online frauds. This digital gap limits their income opportunities and prevents rural products from reaching larger national 
and global markets.

# TARGET USERS
Rural Artisans
First time online sellers
Small village businesses
Housewives selling handmade products

# OUR PROPOSED SOLUTION
Grameen Sathi AI is an AI powered , multi lingual assistant that guides rural sellers step by step through the process of listing and selling products on Amazon and other platforms. It provides voice
based support in simple local languages, suggests optimal pricing, explains shipping and payment processes clearly ,also offering fraud awareness guidance. By simplifying digital commerce, the platform
builds confidence and enables rural entrepreneurs  participate successfully in the world of online marketing.

# KEY FEATURES
Guiding sellers and users through the whole process
Voice and text based AI guidance in local languages
Fraud detection tips and easy techniques to handle them
AI based ideal price suggestions
Simple explanations of shipping and payments

# IMPACT
The launch of Grameen Sathi AI will mark a significant increase in rural participation in e-commerce by building digital confidence and simplifying the selling process. Rural artisans and small 
businesses will gain access to national and global markets, leading to higher and more stable incomes.
The platform will not only improve digital literacy but also reduce the fear of online fraud and encourage women and  first-time entrepreneurs to become online sellers. In the long term, it will
strengthen the rural economy, promote sustainable livelihoods, preserve traditional crafts, and support inclusive growth by connecting villages directly to the digital marketplace.
